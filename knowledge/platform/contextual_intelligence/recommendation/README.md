Version: 1.0.0

# Purpose

The Recommendation domain represents the decision intelligence layer of the Contextual Intelligence Framework.

It synthesizes observations and derived intelligence from multiple contextual domains—including Environment, Vehicle Behaviour, Human Behaviour, Insurance Behaviour, Claims, Fraud, Infrastructure, and Mobility—to generate explainable, prioritized, and actionable recommendations.

Rather than storing business facts, this domain captures **recommended actions**, **decision priorities**, **expected business impact**, **decision confidence**, and **explanations**.

The Recommendation domain transforms contextual understanding into operational decisions.
