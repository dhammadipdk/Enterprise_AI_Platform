# Environment Contextual Intelligence

## Purpose

The Environment domain captures natural and environmental conditions that influence motor insurance risk.

It stores observations and derived intelligence related to weather, climate, terrain, natural hazards, environmental exposure, and atmospheric conditions.

These intelligence objects are consumed by underwriting, pricing, claims, fraud analytics, recommendation engines, and contextual reasoning.

---

## Scope

Included

- Rainfall
- Flooding
- Temperature
- Humidity
- Fog
- Cyclone
- Hailstorm
- Landslide
- Earthquake
- Air Quality
- Visibility
- Wind
- Waterlogging
- Coastal Exposure

Excluded

- Traffic
- Roads
- Infrastructure
- Crime
- Customer Behaviour
