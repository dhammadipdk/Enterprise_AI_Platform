# Human Behavior Contextual Intelligence

Version: 1.0.0

Status: Active

---

# Purpose

The Human Behavior domain models driving style, operational decisions, attention patterns, compliance with traffic rules, and behavioral characteristics that influence accident probability, insurance risk, fraud detection, customer engagement, and personalized recommendations.

Unlike Vehicle Behavior, which focuses on the condition and operation of the vehicle, Human Behavior focuses on the actions and habits of the driver.
