# Infrastructure Contextual Intelligence

Version: 1.0.0

Status: Active

---

# Purpose

The Infrastructure domain models the characteristics and quality of built infrastructure that influence vehicle operation, accident probability, repair accessibility, emergency response, theft risk, and insurance outcomes.

Unlike the Environment domain, Infrastructure focuses on human-built assets and services.

---

# Scope

Included

• Road Infrastructure
• Road Surface Quality
• Road Geometry
• Traffic Control Infrastructure
• Street Lighting
• Parking Infrastructure
• CCTV Coverage
• Fuel Stations
• EV Charging Stations
• Emergency Services
• Road Maintenance
• Bridges
• Tunnels
• Flyovers
• Toll Roads

Excluded

• Weather
• Climate
• Driver Behaviour
• Vehicle Condition
• Traffic Congestion
• Crime
