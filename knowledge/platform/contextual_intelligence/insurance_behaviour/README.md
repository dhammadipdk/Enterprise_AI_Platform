Version: 1.0.0

Status: Active

# Purpose

The Insurance Behavior domain models how an insurance policy behaves throughout its lifecycle. It captures policyholder interactions, premium payment patterns, renewals, endorsements, add-on adoption, coverage utilization, lapse behavior, retention characteristics, and policy servicing activities.

Unlike Human Behavior, which focuses on the driver's actions, Insurance Behavior focuses on the operational characteristics of the insurance contract itself.
