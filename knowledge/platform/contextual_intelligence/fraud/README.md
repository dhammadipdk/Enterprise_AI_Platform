Version: 1.0.0

Status: Active

---

# Purpose

The Fraud domain models observations, intelligence, relationships, and contextual evidence related to potentially fraudulent insurance activities.

It enables proactive fraud detection by combining behavioral, financial, operational, environmental, and historical signals across the insurance ecosystem.

Unlike the Claims domain, which manages claim processing, the Fraud domain focuses on identifying suspicious patterns, prioritizing investigations, reducing false positives, and supporting explainable fraud detection.
