# Claims Contextual Intelligence

Version: 1.0.0

Status: Active

---

# Purpose

The Claims domain models the complete insurance claim lifecycle, from First Notice of Loss (FNOL) through investigation, assessment, settlement, recovery, closure, and post-claim analysis.

It captures operational, financial, legal, behavioral, and contextual information surrounding claims, enabling intelligent claim triage, fraud detection, settlement optimization, customer experience improvement, reserve estimation, and enterprise analytics.

Unlike Insurance Behavior, which models policy lifecycle characteristics, Claims models events occurring after an insured loss.
