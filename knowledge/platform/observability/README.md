# Enterprise AI Observability

Version : 1.0

Status : Active

---

## Purpose

The Observability subsystem provides complete visibility into every activity occurring within the Enterprise AI Platform.

Unlike traditional monitoring, observability captures telemetry, traces, logs, metrics, events, alerts, runtime statistics and business KPIs across the entire cognitive architecture.

The objective is to explain not only what happened, but why it happened.
