# Enterprise Insurance Ontology

Version: 1.0

Status: Active

---

## Purpose

The Enterprise Ontology provides the semantic foundation for the entire InsureAI platform.

It defines enterprise concepts, relationships, inheritance, constraints, properties, and business semantics independent of implementation technologies.

The ontology enables consistent interpretation of data across business domains, contextual intelligence, reasoning engines, knowledge graphs, GraphRAG pipelines, and AI agents.

---

## Objectives

• Establish common enterprise vocabulary

• Standardize semantic definitions

• Enable interoperable knowledge representation

• Support knowledge graph construction

• Support GraphRAG retrieval

• Support explainable reasoning

• Enable enterprise-wide semantic consistency

• Support ontology-driven AI agents
